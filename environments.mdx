Taprail has two environments sharing one base URL. Your API key decides which one you're in.

| | Sandbox (`tr_test_…`) | Live (`tr_live_…`) |
|---|---|---|
| Charges | Simulated, no money moves | Real cards, real money |
| Outcome | Deterministic, by amount (below) | The customer's real authorization |
| Payouts | Marked transferred, no actual transfer | Net settled to your payout account |
| Availability | From signup | After the partner agreement is signed |

Orders, webhooks, keys, and dashboard views are all environment-scoped: a sandbox key only
ever sees sandbox orders.

## Deterministic test amounts

In sandbox, the **last two digits of `amount_kobo`** decide the outcome, so you can exercise
every path in CI without an app in the loop:

| `amount_kobo % 100` | Outcome | `decline_reason` |
|---|---|---|
| `11` | Declined | `sandbox_declined` |
| `22` | Declined (simulated processor timeout) | `sandbox_timeout` |
| anything else | Approved | |

```text
750000 → approved
750011 → declined (sandbox_declined)
750022 → declined (sandbox_timeout)
```

## Going live

Live mode unlocks when you and Taprail have signed the partner agreement. The whole flow
lives in the dashboard under **Account → Go live**, which tracks it as a stepper alongside a
short prerequisite checklist.

**Get these done first** (the checklist on the same page links to each):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Confirm the address you signed up with — live mode is tied to a contactable human.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build and test in sandbox">
    Mint a sandbox key under **Developer → API Keys**, integrate order creation + checkout,
    and exercise the decline and expiry paths with [test amounts](#deterministic-test-amounts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your webhook endpoint">
    Configure the endpoint and signing secrets under **Developer → Webhooks**, and confirm
    you [verify signatures](/webhooks/verify-signatures).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a settlement account">
    Under **Account → Settings**, pick your bank and verify the account. Your net (amount
    minus the Taprail fee) settles there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Then sign the agreement** (the **Account → Go live** stepper):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Business details">
    Fill in your business profile and the name of the person signing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the agreement">
    Review the Taprail Partner agreement and sign it electronically with your legal name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Taprail counter-signs">
    Once you've signed, Taprail counter-signs to execute the agreement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Live mode switches on">
    With the agreement executed, live mode turns on and we email you. Flip the topbar toggle
    to **Live**, mint a `tr_live_` key under **Developer → API Keys**, and switch your
    server's secret.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Fees: your per-order fee is a percentage of the amount with a cap, both shown under
  **Account → Settings**. The fee is computed when the order is created and is
  non-refundable; refunds return the full amount to the customer.
</Note>
