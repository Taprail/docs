---
title: "Loyalty overview"
description: "Reward your customers' card taps with cashback, no integration required."
---

Taprail loyalty pays your customers cashback when they spend with you using their Taprail
card. There's nothing to integrate: no API, no hardware, no till changes. You define a
cashback rule in the [partner dashboard](https://partner.taprail.app), and eligible
transactions start earning within seconds of happening.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Taprail links transactions to your business">
    When a customer taps their Taprail card at your store, the transaction carries your
    business name and merchant category. Taprail matches it to your account using rules set
    up with you during onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your program decides the reward">
    A program is a cashback rule: a percentage, a cap per transaction, and an optional
    total budget. Matching transactions earn according to the active program.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The customer sees it instantly">
    Cashback appears in the customer's Taprail app moments after the tap, attached to your
    business name. That visibility is the loyalty loop: they see your name every time they
    get paid for shopping with you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You watch it work">
    The dashboard shows members, rewarded taps, and cashback spend, live. Every rewarded
    transaction is listed under Rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you control

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| Cashback % | The reward rate, e.g. 5% of the transaction |
| Cap per tap | The most a single transaction can earn, e.g. ₦500 |
| Budget | Optional total spend limit; the program stops rewarding when it's exhausted |
| Status | Pause and resume a program at any time |

Changes apply to future taps immediately. Cashback already earned is never clawed back.

## Who pays for the cashback

You fund the rewards you configure; that's the deal with your customers, made under your
name. Taprail sometimes co-funds launch promotions for new partners. Talk to your account
manager.

## Loyalty and payments are independent

You can run loyalty without accepting Pay with Taprail, and the other way around. They
share one dashboard and complement each other: loyalty brings the customer back, checkout
closes the sale.
